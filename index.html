My Projects

RES-MAP: Establishment Finder with Geo-mapping

Res-Map is a web-based application designed to simplify the discovery of local resorts and accommodations in Echague. It offered features like information management, geo-mapping, statistical analysis, user feedback, and transportation information. The project aimed to create a central platform for Echague's tourism sector, improving the online visibility of local establishments and enhancing the travel experience. Res-Map catered to tourists, establishment owners, and the Tourism Unit of Echague, providing essential information and insights. .

CAS Archiving System

The College of Arts and Science (CAS) Archiving System is a web application that stores files such as personal data, CVs, ISO documents and others for each department. It allows users to upload, download, and delete files based on their account level. Account Level 1 is for the Administrator, Level 2 for the faculty, and Level 3 for program chairs (sub-admins). .
